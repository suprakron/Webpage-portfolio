Quiz Systems for Formative Assessment

Objective

1. To study the principles and methods of creating a test based on Bloom's learning theory in the field of enlightenment.

2. To design the structure of the system for preparing quizzes during classes.

3. To develop a system for preparing tests during classes.

4. To test the functionality of the system, prepare a test during the course.

5. To use the system to prepare the test during the course to create the test during the course.

Project Capabilities
  • 1. Users, consisting of teachers and students, have the ability to use the following:

  • 1.1. Teacher

  • 1.1.1. Subject Management

  • 1) Create the subjects you want to create unlimited quizzes.

  • 2) Delete unwanted subjects.

  • 1.1.2. Quiz Management

  • 1) Add Quiz

  • 1.1) Multiple choice form 2-4 options in which questions and options can include pictures.

  • 1.2) Answer form, questions can include pictures

  • 1.3) Delete Unneeded Quiz

  • 1.4) Editing questions that are guided by the system
